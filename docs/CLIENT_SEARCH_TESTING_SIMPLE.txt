MOJTERMIN — SEARCH TESTING (simple guide)
============================================

How to test:
1. Open the website
2. Type what you need (see examples below)
3. Choose a city
4. Press Search

What you should see:
• A short message explaining what we understood
• A list of hospitals/clinics — OR nothing, if nothing matches that city

When checking results, look at the service names under each hospital:
• They should match what you searched (e.g. MRI → names containing "MRI")
• You should see address and phone for each hospital
